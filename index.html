About Kumar Thermoplast

Pioneering Polystyrene Solutions Since [Year]

Welcome to Kumar Thermoplast, a leading manufacturer and supplier of Expanded Polystyrene (EPS) products. With decades of industry experience, we are dedicated to providing innovative and reliable solutions that meet the diverse needs of our clients.

Our state-of-the-art facility and commitment to quality ensure that every product, from custom-moulded packaging to large insulation sheets, is crafted to the highest standards of performance and durability.

We believe in building lasting partnerships through exceptional customer service, on-time delivery, and a continuous drive for innovation.

Our Core Products

Versatile solutions for every need.

Thermocol Boxes

Lightweight and durable boxes ideal for shipping electronics, pharmaceuticals, and perishable goods. Excellent thermal insulation and shock absorption.

Thermocol Sheets

High-density sheets for construction, insulation, and various industrial applications. Available in multiple thicknesses and sizes.

Thermocol Balls

Tiny, versatile EPS beads used as fillers for bean bags, toys, and lightweight concrete, as well as for decorative purposes.

Custom Moulds

We design and manufacture custom-shaped EPS packaging to provide perfect protection for your unique products.
