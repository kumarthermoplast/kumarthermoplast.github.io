Insulated EPS Boxes

Our insulated boxes are the industry standard for cold chain logistics, offering superior thermal performance to protect perishable goods.

  • Dimensions: Fully Customizable
  • Density: 15 - 30 kg/m³
  • Key Feature: Excellent R-value for temperature stability

High-Density EPS Sheets

Versatile and rigid sheets for building insulation, void filling in construction, and protective packaging layers.

  • Dimensions: Up to 2000x1000mm, custom thickness
  • Density: 10 - 45 kg/m³
  • Key Feature: High compressive strength for load-bearing uses

Bespoke Moulded Solutions

We use your product's CAD data to engineer a perfect-fit packaging solution that eliminates movement and absorbs impact.

  • Dimensions: Engineered to your exact product specifications
  • Density: Variable, based on protection requirements
  • Key Feature: Precision fit drastically reduces in-transit damage
